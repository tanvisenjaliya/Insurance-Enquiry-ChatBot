Plan A: Optimal Benefit

We have our Health Insurance Plan "Optimum Benefit".
Below are the features of our plan-
1. In Patient Hospitalization Covered up to the sum insured. No limits on any category of room even suite is covered
2. Pre & Post Hospitalization is covered for 60 Days & 180 Days
3. All Day care surgeries are covered means - surgeries which do not require any hospitalization such as cateract stones etc there also claim will be settled as per actual's.
4. Domiciliary treatment - Covered upto the the Sum Insured No limits. Only subject to in writing from the treating doctor that the patient is not in a condition to go hospital or beds are not available in the hospital hence domiciliary treatment is need.
5. Ambulance is covered for Rs 2K per hospitalization.
6. Daily cash for 800/- per day max up to RS 4800 for choosing a shared accommodation
7. Air Ambulance - 2.5 Lakhs per hospitalization
8. Restore benefit -- 100% of Sum Inured. Irrespective of if the base sum insured is exhausted or not.
9. Cumulative bonus - 50% of the base sum insured upto 100%. If there are two continuous claim free years, the sum insured will be just double in two years.
10. Health check ups can be availed for 5000.
11. Organ Donor expenses covered upto sum insured.
